**Overview**: Zo House collaborates with visionary groups to amplify creativity and connection. From events in the Schelling Point to projects in the Zo Studio IRL, our partners help build a global community for life design.

**Why Partner?**

* **Global Network**: Connect across Zo Houses in Bangalore, San Francisco, and more.
* **Vibrant Spaces**: Use Multiverse or Schelling Point for events and workshops.
* **Effect**: Sparks innovation and community, enriching lives.
